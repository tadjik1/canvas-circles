This small application allows you to draw geometrical shapes. You can choose any 3 points on the screen (red circles are drawn in each point). If there are 3 points on the screen, 2 shapes are additionally drawn automatically: blue parallelogram (having 3 of its corners in these 3 points) and yellow circle (having the same area and centre of mass as the parallelogram).

Since there are 3 parallelograms from 3 points, you can switch from one to another.

You can reset all figures by pressing "reset" button on the top of the screen any time.

There is also a table with all numerical values presenting the current scene.

And you could drag any red circle on the screen.
